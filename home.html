Common Problems Faced by Refugees:

  1. Legal and documentation issues: Refugees often face difficulties in obtaining legal status and documentation in their host countries. This can limit their access to employment, education, and social services, making it harder for them to rebuild their lives.
  2. Unsafe migration routes and human trafficking: Refugees may encounter dangerous migration routes and become vulnerable to human trafficking. They require accurate information about safe migration options and potential risks.
  3. Mental health and psychosocial support: Refugees often experience trauma and stress due to displacement and require accessible and culturally sensitive mental health services to address their mental well-being.
